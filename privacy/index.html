2. Why we collect it (legal basis under GDPR)

  • Contract performance — we need your email and payment confirmation to deliver a license/serial code you purchased.
  • Legitimate interest — waitlist signups, so we can tell you when a product you're interested in launches or changes price.
  • Consent — for Oli-Locator's optional data your account may hold beyond what's strictly needed to run the product, and for any future marketing emails beyond transactional/waitlist ones (you can withdraw consent anytime).

3. Self-hosted tools — a fundamentally different privacy model

For OliOps Suite, OliCommerce Stack, OliFlow Engine, and OliConnect: the data your business processes through the tool never reaches us. You run the software on infrastructure you choose. We are not a data processor for that content in the GDPR sense — you are the data controller for whatever your CRM records, invoices, or automations contain, and your own hosting provider is your data processor. This is genuinely different from most SaaS privacy policies, and it's worth understanding: it means stronger data control for you, but it also means we cannot help recover data you lose if you don't back it up yourself (see the Security page).

5. How long we keep data

  • Self-hosted tool data: entirely up to you — it's on your infrastructure, retained as long as you keep it there.
  • Waitlist emails: kept until you unsubscribe or request deletion, or up to 24 months of inactivity, whichever comes first.
  • Purchase records: kept as long as required for tax/accounting law (typically 6-7 years depending on jurisdiction), then deleted.
  • Oli-Locator account data: kept while your subscription is active, plus 30 days after cancellation (in case you want to reactivate), then deleted upon request or after 90 days of cancellation.

7. Cookies

This marketing website does not set its own tracking cookies. Third-party widgets embedded on specific pages (Brevo's signup form, Stripe/PayPal checkout buttons) may set their own cookies only when you interact with them — see our cookie banner for the current, up-to-date list. If we add analytics in the future, this section and the cookie banner will be updated to name the specific tool and what it tracks before it goes live.

8. International data transfers

If you're located outside the country where our hosting providers or payment processors operate, your data may be transferred internationally to process your request or payment. Stripe and PayPal are both certified under recognized international data transfer frameworks (e.g. EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework where applicable) — see their respective privacy policies for specifics, as they are independent data controllers for payment processing.

9. Children's privacy

Oli Tools are business tools intended for business owners, freelancers, and professionals. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 16. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we'll delete it promptly.

10. Changes to this policy

If we materially change how we collect or use data, we'll update the "Last updated" date above and, where required, notify active account holders directly.
