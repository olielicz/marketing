Questions, answered honestly

Do I need to know how to code?

No. If you'd rather not self-host, our managed hosting tier ($19/mo) runs everything for you — zero setup required on your end.

Is this a subscription? How does billing work?

Yes — $149/year, billed annually, cancel anytime before your renewal date. It's an annual plan rather than a one-time lifetime purchase, which funds ongoing updates, security fixes, and support instead of a single upfront payment. The optional $19/mo managed-hosting add-on is separate and only for people who'd rather not self-host.

How does the serial code / 5-device limit work?

Every subscription comes with one serial code, activatable on up to 5 devices at a time. Setting up a new machine? Deactivate an old device from your account (or self-service, no email needed) to free up a slot. This isn't hardware-locked — it's a simple, fair device count, not copy-protection.

What if it's not the right fit for my business?

30-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. We'd rather refund you than have you stuck with a tool that doesn't fit.

Why is it so much cheaper than 4 separate tools?

Because there's no venture-backed sales team or ad budget baked into the price — just the software, priced once a year instead of four separate monthly bills stacking on top of each other.

The core tools are MIT-licensed — why do I need a serial code at all?

Good question, and we'd rather be upfront: the underlying code is open-source, so nothing here is unbreakable copy-protection. Your subscription pays for the packaged, ready-to-run build, ongoing updates and security fixes, priority support, and a simple way to track your own devices — not an exclusive license to code you couldn't otherwise read. We're trusting our customers to license fairly in exchange for keeping this affordable for everyone.
