Questions, answered honestly

Do I need to know how to code?

No. If you'd rather not self-host, our managed hosting tier ($19/mo, optional and separate from the lifetime license) runs everything for you — zero setup required on your end.

Is this really a one-time payment?

Yes. $299 once for lifetime access to all three tools, including every future update. There's no renewal, no recurring bill for the license itself. The optional $19/mo managed-hosting add-on is only for people who want us to host and maintain the server for them.

How does the serial code / 5-device limit work?

Your purchase includes one serial code, activatable on up to 5 devices at a time. Setting up a new machine? Deactivate an old device from your account (self-service, no email needed) to free up a slot. This isn't hardware-locked — it's a simple, fair device count, not copy-protection.

What if it's not the right fit for my business?

30-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. We'd rather refund you than have you stuck with a tool that doesn't fit.

How do I pay, and is it safe?

Checkout runs through Stripe and PayPal — the two most widely used payment processors in the world, supporting cards, digital wallets, and local payment methods across 100+ countries. We never see or store your card details.

The core tools are MIT-licensed — why do I need a serial code at all?

Good question, and we'd rather be upfront: the underlying code is open-source, so nothing here is unbreakable copy-protection. Your purchase pays for the packaged, ready-to-run build, ongoing updates and security fixes, priority support, and a simple way to track your own devices — not an exclusive license to code you couldn't otherwise read. We're trusting our customers to license fairly in exchange for keeping this affordable for everyone.
