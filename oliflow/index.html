Questions, answered honestly

How does the serial code / 5-device limit work?

Your purchase includes one serial code, activatable on up to 5 devices at a time. Moving to a new server or laptop? Deactivate the old device to free a slot — no email required.

How do I pay, and is it safe?

Checkout runs through Stripe and PayPal — the two most widely used payment processors in the world, supporting cards, wallets, and local payment methods across 100+ countries. We never see or store your card details.

The core engine is MIT-licensed — why a purchase price at all?

The underlying code is open-source and readable by anyone. Your purchase funds the packaged build, the prebuilt template pack, ongoing updates, and support — not an exclusive claim on code you could otherwise read for free. We're upfront about that tradeoff.
