## Full launch post body

**Hey Product Hunt 👋**

I'm olielicz. I built OliConnect after realizing how much time social media managers spend manually reformatting the same winning post for 4 different platforms.

**What it does:**
- 🔗 **Connect once** — log into your social accounts inside the OliConnect browser extension
- 📊 **Auto-detect top posts** — OliConnect identifies your best-performing content across every connected account
- ♻️ **Recycle automatically** — top posts get reformatted and reposted across your other accounts

**Why it's different:**
- **One-time payment.** $29 once, not a recurring social-media-tool subscription.
- **Your credentials stay yours.** Session-based, not stored server-side.
- **Built for solo operators and small agencies**, not enterprise social suites with per-seat pricing.

**Who it's for:** social media managers, small agencies, and solo creators juggling multiple platforms who want their best content to work harder without manual re-posting.

Happy to answer anything in the comments today. 🙏
