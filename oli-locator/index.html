Questions, answered honestly

Can I use my own phone/calling app?

Yes — that's by design. Tapping a number in Oli-Locator opens your device's default dialer via a standard tap-to-call link, so whatever calling app you already use (your carrier's dialer, a VoIP app, a dedicated business-line app) just works. No forced platform, no bundled calling subscription.

Is there a device limit?

No — Oli-Locator is a hosted, login-based app (not something you download or self-host), so log in from as many devices as you personally use day to day. There's no separate serial code or device cap for this product.

How do I pay, and is it safe?

Checkout runs through Stripe and PayPal — the two most widely used payment processors in the world. Your card is billed monthly and you can cancel anytime from your account settings.
